Ferro Slider jQuery Plugin

This plugins allows you to organize the contents of you website in a unusual and cool way and navigate through them with a sliding effect.
The final effect is similar to Mac's or Linux multiple desktops.
You can position content as you want: by row, by column or using a custom displacement.

FerroSlider is really simple to use, due to the multiple available plugin's options.

For a complete documentation, go to: http://www.alessandroferrini.it/lab/jQueryPlugins/ferroSlider/docs

Check demos available at http://www.alessandroferrini.it/lab/jQueryPlugins/ferroSlider/docs/index.php#demos.
